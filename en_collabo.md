## eduroam Agreement

**eduroam** is a non-profit movement that started in 2002 in the European Union with the aim of jointly using Wi-Fi for research and educational institutions. Currently, 4,885 organizations in 85 countries, including the United States and Japan, are involved.


Since 2012 [KISTI(Korea Institute of Science and Technology Information)](http://www.kisti.re.kr) has started eduroam servcie in Korea through mutual agreement with [Global eduroam Committee](https://www.eduroam.org). As of January 2018, 50 institutions in Korea are participating in eduroam.
